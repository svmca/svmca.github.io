Pet Rescue NI

Find your new best friend

Rehoming Unwanted and Abandoned Cats and Dogs since 2017

Pet Rescue NI aims to help the unwanted, neglected and abandoned and give them a chance of happiness. We currently have many animals with us who need 5 star loving homes. We also have several animal that will remain with us for longer periods of time due to their specific needs.

We are entirely self funded and run by a very small team of dedicated volunteers. All money raised goes directly towards the feeding and caring of the animals, this also includes hefty veterinary bills for those that have been mistreated or become ill. Every penny you give goes directly to helping these animals and saving more.
